1. Water

Role: Solvent Function: Acts as the primary base and solvent for the formulation, dissolving other ingredients and ensuring an even consistency.

2. Cetearyl Alcohol

Role: Emulsifier and Emollient Function: Helps to stabilize and thicken the formulation. It also provides a smooth, soft feel on the skin.

3. Mineral Oil

Role: Emollient and Moisturizer Function: Forms a barrier on the skin's surface to prevent moisture loss, keeping the skin hydrated.

4. Potassium Thioglycolate

Role: Depilatory Agent Function: Breaks down the protein structure of hair, facilitating its removal from the skin surface.

5. Calcium Hydroxide

Role: pH Adjuster Function: Maintains the pH balance of the product to ensure its effectiveness and safety for the skin.

6. Ceteareth-20

Role: Emulsifier Function: Helps to blend water and oil components together, maintaining the stability of the product.

7. Talc

Role: Absorbent and Filler Function: Absorbs moisture and oil, giving a smooth texture and reducing greasiness.

8. Glycerin

Role: Humectant Function: Attracts water to the skin, keeping it hydrated and soft.

9. Fragrance

Role: Scent Function: Provides a pleasant smell to the product, enhancing the user experience.

10. Polyethylene

Role: Thickener and Scrubbing Agent Function: Adds thickness to the formulation and can also serve as a gentle exfoliant.

11. Sodium Gluconate

Role: Chelating Agent Function: Binds metal ions to improve the stability and efficacy of the product.

12. Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice

Role: Skin Conditioning Agent Function: Soothes and moisturizes the skin, providing anti-inflammatory and healing benefits.

13. Citric Acid

Role: pH Adjuster and Preservative Function: Adjusts the pH of the product and acts as a mild preservative to extend shelf life.

14. Potassium Sorbate

Role: Preservative Function: Prevents the growth of mold, yeast, and fungi, ensuring the product's longevity and safety.

15. Sodium Benzoate

Role: Preservative Function: Inhibits the growth of bacteria, yeast, and fungi, helping to maintain the product's integrity and shelf life. Summary These ingredients work synergistically to create a stable, effective product that moisturizes and conditions the skin while facilitating hair removal (due to the depilatory action of Potassium Thioglycolate and the pH adjustment by Calcium Hydroxide). Emollients like Mineral Oil and Cetearyl Alcohol ensure the skin remains hydrated and smooth. Preservatives like Potassium Sorbate and Sodium Benzoate maintain the product's safety and efficacy over time, while humectants like Glycerin attract moisture, keeping the skin soft and supple.
